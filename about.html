Welcome to Apex Public School

Apex Public School located in Anjani Bazar, Parsa, Saran provides quality education from Play Group to Class 8. Our institution focuses on academic excellence, discipline, and character development for students.

The school aims to provide strong educational foundation and prepare students for future competitive challenges.

Principal Message

Education builds confidence, discipline and strong character. Our goal is to provide equal learning opportunities to every child including economically weaker students. We focus on academic excellence and holistic development.
