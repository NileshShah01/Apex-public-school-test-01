Welcome to Apex Public School

Apex Public School, located in Anjani Bazar, Parsa, Saran, Bihar, provides quality education to students from Play Group to Class 8. The school was established with the vision of empowering young minds through modern education and strong moral values.

Our school is recognized with UDISE code 10171706503 and registration number 2111449. We provide a supportive environment where students grow academically, socially and emotionally.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide affordable and quality education to every child. We support students from all economic backgrounds and guide them to succeed in academic studies and future competitive examinations.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading educational institution known for academic excellence, discipline, and character building. We aim to develop responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Values

  • Integrity and honesty in every action
  • Excellence in academic performance
  • Respect for teachers, parents and community
  • Compassion and care for others

Why Choose Apex Public School

  • Experienced and dedicated teaching staff
  • Individual attention to every student
  • Smart classroom teaching methods
  • Strong focus on discipline and moral values
  • Safe and secure learning environment
  • Affordable education for all
  • Regular parent teacher communication
  • Sports and co curricular activities
